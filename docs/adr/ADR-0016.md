# ADR-0016: Test corpus governance: synthetic-only in CI

Status: PROPOSED
Date: 2026-07-06
Register: D16

## Context
threat-model.md D16. Replay CI needs recorded runs; real runs contain the owner's actual life.

## Decision
Repo/CI corpora are synthetic, produced by the corpus generator; real-run replay is local-only; secret/PII scan gates corpus commits; corpora from every release are retained as the upcaster ratchet.

## Consequences
The generator becomes a Stage-1 deliverable (roadmap E2); privacy incidents via test data are structurally excluded.
