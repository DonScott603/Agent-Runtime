# ADR-0007: Resolution: specificity-first, deny always global

Status: ACCEPTED
Date: 2026-07-06
Register: D7

## Context
RFC-0003 D7. Global deny-override makes broad ask rules kill narrow allows, driving approval fatigue and broad-allow workarounds.

## Decision
Most specific matching rule wins (level order, then qualifiers bound, then matcher tightness); ties resolve deny > ask > allow; a deny at any level wins regardless of specificity. Total order; every decision records the winning rule and candidate set.

## Consequences
Narrow allows work under broad asks; deny remains fail-safe; resolution is explainable and property-tested (metamorphic suite).
